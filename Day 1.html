Overall Challenges Faced During Training

Throughout the 28 days of training in AI and ML, several challenges emerged that tested my understanding and skills:

  • Understanding Core Concepts: Grasping the fundamental concepts of Python programming, AI, and ML was initially overwhelming due to the rapid pace of the course.
  • Library Usage: Navigating various libraries such as NumPy, Pandas, and Scikit-learn was challenging, as each library has its own functions and syntax that required time to learn.
  • Data Preprocessing: Cleaning and preprocessing data for ML models proved to be tedious and error-prone, necessitating attention to detail and a clear understanding of the data.
  • Model Evaluation: Understanding different evaluation metrics (like accuracy, precision, recall, F1-score) and their implications for model performance was complex.
  • Project Integration: Integrating all learned concepts into a cohesive end-to-end project, specifically the movie recommender system, was a significant challenge that required effective project management and debugging skills.
  • Presentation Skills: Effectively presenting my project to peers and instructors made me nervous, highlighting a need for improved public speaking and communication skills.

Despite these challenges, each obstacle provided an opportunity for growth, enhancing my understanding and practical skills in AI and ML.
